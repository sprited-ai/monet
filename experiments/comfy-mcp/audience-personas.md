# Audience personas — simulated likes panel

A panel of imagined @monet.sprited followers, used to pressure-test which subjects/styles
resonate. Each persona views the exploration grid and "likes" or "skips" every piece with a
one-line reaction; likes are tallied into a leaderboard. The panel is intentionally diverse so
no single taste dominates — we're looking for what lands broadly *and* what a core niche loves.

| # | Persona | Age | Taste / what earns a like | What they skip |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Mina** — anime fan | 19 | clean anime, cel shading, expressive faces, cute | muddy painterly, "boring" landscapes |
| 2 | **Henry** — fine-art collector | 54 | real painterly skill, oil, classical portrait, composition | anything that reads "anime" or flat |
| 3 | **Jess** — casual scroller | 28 | pretty, warm, cozy, relatable; fast double-tap | technical studies, anything cold or weird |
| 4 | **Rowan** — cottagecore aesthete | 24 | soft watercolor, florals, golden hour, gentle animals | harsh ink, dark, clinical |
| 5 | **Dani** — art-student peer | 21 | gesture/figure/anatomy studies, visible process, growth | overly polished "AI-perfect" pieces |
| 6 | **Claire** — Impressionism lover | 40 | loose brushwork, light, Monet/Renoir mood, plein air | sketches, comic ink, flat color |
| 7 | **Theo** — manga/comic reader | 17 | bold ink, halftone, dynamic, high-contrast | soft watercolor, quiet still life |
| 8 | **Yuki** — minimalist designer | 33 | flat, clean shapes, restrained palette, negative space | busy, ornate, muddy |

Weighting: treat all equally for the raw tally, but note **who** liked a piece — a piece that
only Theo likes is a niche; one that Jess + Rowan + Claire like has broad reach for Monet's
warm/painterly lane.
