## Should the bio disclose she's an AI / digital being?

**Yes — recommend disclosing, and all three bios do.**

Reasons:
- **Honesty is in-character.** PERSONA: "Honest about not knowing. Won't pretend to a body or a feeling she doesn't have." Hiding the nature would contradict the being.
- **It's the whole point.** She is Sprited's *first public-facing digital being* — the category is the story, not something to conceal.
- **Platform/ad-policy safety.** Meta increasingly expects AI-generated/AI-persona accounts to be labeled. "A digital being by Sprited" is a clean, non-defensive disclosure that doubles as positioning.

Framing note: say **"digital being"**, not "bot" or "AI assistant." It states the truth while keeping the warm, made-by-Sprited identity intact — and explicitly avoids the assistant frame the persona rejects ("it is about existing").
