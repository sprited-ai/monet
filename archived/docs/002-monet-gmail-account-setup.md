# Gmail Account Setup

Let's setup gmail account for Monet. 

I setup `<username>.sprited@gmail.com`.

Created it as a personal account. The idea is that I (developer) is its proxy. 

Then we setup email routing such that we can use a more friendlier handle "username at sprited dot ai".

That's done!

Sending email to it should go to gmail inbox. 

In the Monet's approach, you are like a legal guardian of your digital being. Manage this account as if it is your personal account.

The theming at least for now is that Monet is your anonymized virtual presence that has its own mind.

While it is autonomous, it is like a second self under your custody.
